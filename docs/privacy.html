Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Stable Diffusion AI handles information when you use the app.

Stable Diffusion AI (SDAI) is an open-source app built by Dmitriy Moroz. The app itself does not include ads or telemetry, and the maintainer does not collect personal information from the app for analytics or advertising.

Information Stored On Your Device

SDAI stores app settings, provider configuration, generated images, gallery metadata, selected local models, and optional logs locally on your device. API keys, server URLs, and local model paths are used to run the features you configure in the app.

You control when generated images are saved, shared, exported, or deleted. Gallery export and image sharing happen only when you start those actions.

AI Providers

SDAI can connect to providers you choose, including AUTOMATIC1111, SwarmUI, AI Horde, Hugging Face, OpenAI, Stability AI, Fal.ai, ArliAI, Android local ONNX, Google AI MediaPipe, Android local SDXL, Silicon Diffusion Core ML, and Silicon Diffusion PrismML Bonsai.

When you use a remote provider, prompts, input images, generation settings, API keys, and related request data may be sent to that provider or to your own server. Those services process data under their own terms and privacy policies. Local ONNX, MediaPipe, local SDXL, Core ML, and Bonsai generation are designed to run on device after the selected model is available.

Device Permissions

SDAI may request device permissions for features you choose to use:

  • Camera access for taking an input image for Img2Img.
  • Storage or media access for reading, saving, exporting, and sharing images.
  • Notification permission for background generation progress.

Denying a permission may disable the related feature, but the rest of the app can continue to work where possible.

Reports And Logs

SDAI can help you report a problem. A report or support email is sent only after you start that action. Depending on the report flow, it may include the text you write, the selected report reason, current provider type, selected model identifier, an image you attach, or a local log file.

Cookies

SDAI does not use browser cookies for app analytics or advertising. External websites linked from the app or this site may use cookies according to their own policies.

Links To Other Sites

The app and website link to third-party services, stores, documentation, and community pages. These sites are not operated by the maintainer, so review their policies before using them.

Children's Privacy

SDAI is not directed to children under 13. The maintainer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes are published on this page.
